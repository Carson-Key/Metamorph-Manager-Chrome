How to use MetaMorph

This extension unfortunatly only works on a popped out chacter sheet and will only change the misc/other field of stats/skills (excluding close combat, expertise, ranged attack, and iniative), currently

In order for this extension to work you will need to layout your powers so that the extension knows how to parse it

First you will need to have a power for each of your metamorph stat sets

In this power the only thing that matters is the description, this extension won't read the title/ranks/ects

Make sure that each indivdual power of the set (or power array) is on a new line, this extension will consider each line a seperate stat-increase/power within the set

example:

- Increased Strength 1 (2 pp)
- Increased Fighting 1 (2 pp)
- Increased Agility 1 (2 pp)
- Increased Stamina 1 (2 pp)
- Increased Dodge 2 (2 pp)
- Increased Fortitude 4 (4 pp)
- Increased Toughness 4 (4 pp)
- Increased Will 7 (7 pp)
- Increased Parry 1 (1 pp)
- Increased Treatment 8 (4 pp)
- Healing 10 (70 pp)
  Area (30ft sphere), persistent, restorative, resurrection, and selectie

The only words/characters that matter in this description is the word increased (case insensitive), the stat it increases (also case insensitive), and the rank (the value that the stat will increase)

Make sure that the stat is spelled as it is in roll20, otherwise that stat will not change

Also make sure that these three words/numbers are in the exact order above (Increased, then skill, finally value)

The final thing you need is an identifier that tells the extension the name and that the power is a metamorph skill set

This identifier is just "metamorph {Name you want to give that set}", in that order (case insensitive)

example:

- Metamorph Healing <---- this one
- Increased Strength 1 (2 pp)
- Increased Fighting 1 (2 pp)
- Increased Agility 1 (2 pp)
- Increased Stamina 1 (2 pp)
- Increased Dodge 2 (2 pp)
- Increased Fortitude 4 (4 pp)
- Increased Toughness 4 (4 pp)
- Increased Will 7 (7 pp)
- Increased Parry 1 (1 pp)
- Increased Treatment 8 (4 pp)
- Healing 10 (70 pp)
  Area (30ft sphere), persistent, restorative, resurrection, and selectie

Once you have all of these things set up, and you popout your character there will be new buttons next to Stats&Skills/Powers,Advantages&Equipment/Options

Clicking these buttons will set the misc/other field of your character stats/skills to whatever you defined in the metamorph's power
